I normally don't play a lot of FPS's, RPGs are usually my go-to, but I must say I was really surprised by how much I enjoyed playing Dying Light.

This game has RPG elements and plays like 'Mirror's Edge'. It has 'TONS' of quests! I easily spent over 20 hours just on side quests alone and I barely dented the main quest. If you are familiar with the Fallout series it plays a lot like that! The main difference is that in Dying Light you can climb walls and jump up onto different levels of buildings.

In the beginning of the game, it's difficult - like super difficult. You try to kill everything (I quickly learned the best way it to just jump on buildings and avoid zombies as much as you can. They will keep coming.) Once you level up and build up some skills It gets a lot easier.

One thing that surprised me is that no one ever mentions this game when they are listing off 'top # CO-OP game'. This game is super fun as a multiplayer CO-OP. You need to constantly watch your partners back while the hordes of zombies come in while they lockpick vans and locked boxes to find medkits and cool items etc.

Overall I think this is one of my fave games of the year.
